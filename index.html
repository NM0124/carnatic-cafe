Our Heritage

Flavours of Southern Soil

At Carnatic Cafe, we serve cuisine that carries the warmth of traditional Karnataka homes. Every single recipe is preserved and passed down through generations, bringing unmatched authenticity to Gurugram.

Using stone-ground batters, pure rich ghee, fresh hand-plucked herbs, and spices sourced straight from southern fields, we honor our roots and promise premium quality in every bite.

Dinner Cuisines

Savoury Traditional Recipes

A Bangalore-Style Sunrise

Fuel your morning with pillowy soft, hot steamed idlis melting with pure home-churned white butter. Dip them in aromatic, fresh coconut chutney and spicy sambar. Round off your breakfast with a steaming brass tumbler of our artisan filter coffee, frothed dynamically to wake up your senses.

Artisanal Mid-Day Brunch

Brunch calls for something crispy yet hearty. Discover our legendary Benne Dosa, with its thick, golden, butter-cooked shell and airy interior, filled with hand-mashed, spiced potato bhaji. A truly comforting and luxury dining experience before the afternoon set.

A Culinary Karnataka Feast

Our complete afternoon meals are designed with balance. Enjoy bowls of slow-simmered Rasam, hearty Sambar, seasonal dry subjis, curd, papad, and fresh steamed rice. Clean, wholesome, and completely vegetarian, cooked fresh daily to revitalize your body.

Bespoke Evening Coffee Hour

Unwind in our cozy modern ambience as the evening rolls in. Sip on hand-ground premium Filter Coffee alongside fresh, hot Paddu (crisp steamed rice-lentil balls made in special cast iron pans), served with rich tomato and coconut dip. The ideal post-work pick-me-up.

Sophisticated Evening Dining

As dusk settles, gather with family for a celebratory dinner. Explore our complete selection of regional specials, from Malleswaram Dosa loaded with signature podi to sweet Kesari Bath. A premium, leisurely dining experience marked by warm, legendary hospitality.
